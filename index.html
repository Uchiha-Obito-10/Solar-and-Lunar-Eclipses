Differences between Solar and Lunar Eclipses




How many times it has been that you forgot the differences between a solar eclipse and a lunar eclipse

Solar Eclipse

Get it now? Don't worry I will explain further






Lunar eclipse

  1. Lunar eclipse happen when the earth covers up the Moon.
  2. Lunar is Luna, and Luna translated in latin means Moon.
  3. If you wanna look at a Lunar eclipse you can look with
    your bare eyes.
  4. In a lunar eclipse the order is Sun Earth Moon and in short SEM.

Solar eclipse

  1. Solar eclipse happen when the moon casts a shadow on the Earth.
  2. Solar is sol, and sol translated in latin means Sun.
  3. If you wanna look at a solar eclipse you can't look with
    your bare eyes. You need special glasses so you don't burn your retina.
  4. In a solar eclipse the order is Sun Moon Earth and in short SME.
